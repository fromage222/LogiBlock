<objective>
Implement the server-side foundation of simplified browser-reload recovery: players who drop mid-game are marked `disconnected: true` for a short grace period, `advanceTurn` skips them, `getPublicState` exposes the flag to clients, and the lobby auto-cleans when every remaining player is disconnected. When a player disconnects mid-game, a `game:stateUpdate` is broadcast so other players see the dimmed badge immediately.

Purpose: Phase 15 CONTEXT.md locks a simplified model -- no 30s timer, no notifications, no overlay. This plan wires the server concerns: disconnected flag on players, turn skipping, all-disconnect cleanup, and immediate state broadcast to other players.

Output:
- `server/src/game.js`: `advanceTurn` skip-loop with cycle guard, `addPlayer`/`createLobby` initialize `disconnected: false`, `getPublicState` exposes `disconnected` flag.
- `server/src/socket.js`: `disconnecting` handler sets `player.disconnected = true` and broadcasts `game:stateUpdate` during game phase; `reconnectRoom` handler clears it; hold-timer callback deletes lobby when all disconnected.
</objective>
